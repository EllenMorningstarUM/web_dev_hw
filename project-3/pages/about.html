The Ladies Behind F#CKED

Corrine Fisher

Corrine is 1/2 of Sorry About Lastnight comedy duo. You can find her on the streets of Harlem walking her precious pup Alfred or on the stage at Just for Laughs comedy club. Notably Corrine is the gut busting blunt force on the GWF podcast. She made sure she didn't hold back in the book.

"Until I began recording Guys We Fucked podcast, I really had no idea just how bad people felt themselves. As I like to bring up often, because I think it's one of her greatest comedic quotes, my mom has told me on more than one occasion that I have "too much self-confidence."

Krystyna Hutchinson

Krystyna is the other 1/2 of Sorry About Lastnight comedy duo. You can find Krystyna with her long-time boyfriend Stephen trying out random sex clubs while on tour or interviewing possible threesome participants. She is known for her large voice and very concerning tone.

"Not only has my glass always been half-full, it's straight-up overflowing onto the floor. I'm the type of optimist who naively overestimates what I'm capable of. I was lucky enough to be raised by two parents I love and respect beyond words."
