Morningstar Mouthwatering Biscuits

By: Ellen Morningstar

The Best Biscuits

The Morningstar Biscuits have been passed down generation through generation. Once being a baseline for fry bread; this recipe is now a request for every cowboy's meal.

The Recipe:

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: 15 - 20 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 21/2 cups of All-Purpose flour
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1/3 cup of real butter
  • 1tbsp of baking powder
  • 1tbsp of white sugar
  • 1tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1tbsp of dried rosemary
  • 1tsp of salt
  • 1tsp of pepper

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Grease baking sheet with butter.
  3. Add all dry ingredients into a large bowl.
  4. Chop up butter to resemble crumbs (cold butter works best).
  5. Add milk and butter to the dry ingredients and mix until moist and sticky.
  6. Flour your hard surface and kneed the dough until no longer sticky.
  7. Roll out biscuit dough to the desired thickness.
  8. Cut out biscuits with cookie cutter or a glass cup.
  9. Place biscuites side-by-side on greased baking sheet.
  10. Bake at 4250 for 15 to 18 minutes.
  11. Remove from oven and brush the biscuit tops with melted butter.
